Sometimes people misinterpret the notion of fiduciary in business affairs, interpreting this person as nominee shareholder or director and therefore understand fiduciary services as nominee services. However this is not quite right.

Fiduciary relationships in business affairs are relationships based on trust to the fiduciary. Our team consist of professional counsels and we personally guarantee loyalty to our clients and absolute diligence.
